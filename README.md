# Materials Science · Interactive

**Structure, properties, and processing of engineering materials**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Atomic structure and bonding
- Crystal structures and defects
- Mechanical properties and failure analysis
- Phase diagrams and transformations
- Metals, ceramics, polymers, composites
- Electronic, magnetic, and optical properties

## References

- William D. Callister Jr. & David G. Rethwisch, *Materials Science and Engineering: An Introduction* (10th ed., Wiley)
- James F. Shackelford, *Introduction to Materials Science for Engineers* (8th ed., Pearson)
- Robert W. Cahn & Peter Haasen, *Physical Metallurgy* (4th ed., Elsevier)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
